Gideon Grossman

Full stack web developer who enjoys building web applications for clients across diverse industries.

I earned a bachelors degree in mechanical engineering with honors from Princeton University as well as certificates in environmental studies and sustainable energy. I began my career as a mechanical engineer designing military robots, navigation instruments, genetic sequencers and smart home appliances. I later moved into firmware engineering, writing C code for bluetooth enabled smart locks and high end electronic art installations. Since 2017, I have been been working remotely as a full stack web developer, building SPA web apps in Vue.js, Java and PostgreSQL. I specialize in building B2B tools for handling and visualizing large datasets.
